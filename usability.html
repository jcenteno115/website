Usability and Accessibility for this Website

I tried to make this website as accessibile as possible to people who visit it. I tried to make it as simple as I could, that way
age and computer knowledge doesn't affect the person's enjoyment of the website. I don't have too much experience with
creating webites, but this was intended to work in all browsers.

Visuals

For the images and the video in the website I tried to make them large, but not ot he extent that they take up the entire screen
and felt that the sizes were reasonable. I also created alt text for every image that way people who are visually impared will
be able to tell what is on the screen.

Navigation

I really wanted navigation to be as simple as possible that way nobody can lost too easily. I decided to put the navigation bar at
the very top of the homepage so people will instantly see it and the links. I designed it so whenever the person hovers over
the link it will turn a different color to let them know which links they are about click. To let them know which page they
recently visited the underline disappers.
