Usability and Accessibility for this Website

I tried to make this website as accessible as possible to people who visit it. I tried to make it as simple as I could, that way age and computer knowledge doesn't affect the person's enjoyment of the website. I don't have too much experience with creating websites, but this was intended to work in all browsers.

Visuals

For the images and the video in the website I tried to make them large, but not ot he extent that they take up the entire screen and felt that the sizes were reasonable. I also created alt text for every image that way people who are visually impaired will be able to tell what is on the screen.

Navigation

I really wanted navigation to be as simple as possible that way nobody can lost too easily. I decided to put the navigation bar at the very top of the homepage so people will instantly see it and the links. I designed it so whenever the person hovers over the link it will turn a different color to let them know which links they are about click. To let them know which page they recently visited the underline disappers.

Printing

I made so that printing would be easy, by that having everything in random locations. I made the alignments as simple as I could get them. To make sure the printing was good I previewed the page and everything was in order.

Future Improvements

The website will be going through several reworks in the near future to make things more appealing and possibly easier navigation, so if there's any constructive please contact me.
